Our Medical Services

Comprehensive emergency transport solutions for all medical needs

Basic Life Support

Essential medical transport with trained EMTs and basic equipment for stable patients.

  • First aid equipment
  • Oxygen supply
  • Stretcher & wheelchair
  • Emergency medication
Advanced Life Support

Critical care transport with paramedics, cardiac monitoring, and advanced interventions.

  • Cardiac monitor/defibrillator
  • Ventilator support
  • IV medications
  • Advanced airway management
Neonatal & Pediatric Transport

Specialized transport for infants and children with pediatric-trained staff and equipment.

  • Neonatal incubator
  • Pediatric ventilators
  • Specialized monitoring
  • Child life specialist
Air Ambulance

Rapid long-distance transport via helicopter or fixed-wing aircraft for critical patients.

  • Inter-hospital transfers
  • Remote area access
  • ICU-level care in flight
  • Nationwide coverage
Mortuary & Funeral Services

Dignified transportation for deceased individuals with temperature-controlled vehicles.

  • Body freezer van
  • Funeral assistance
  • Religious rites support
  • Long-distance transport
Event Medical Coverage

On-site medical teams and ambulances for festivals, sports events, and large gatherings.

  • First aid stations
  • Rapid response teams
  • Emergency transport
  • Mass casualty preparedness
